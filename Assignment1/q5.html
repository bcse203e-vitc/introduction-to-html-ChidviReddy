The book The Midnight Library by Matt Haig explores the concept of parallel lives in a world of infinite choices. The protagonist, Nora Seed, finds herself in a mysterious library between life and death. There, she is given the chance to explore the outcomes of her regrets in different realities. Through her journey, she discovers the value of the ordinary moments and the importance of making authentic choices. Matt Haig masterfully blends philosophical reflection with a touching narrative.
